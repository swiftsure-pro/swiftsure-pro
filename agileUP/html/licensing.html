The licensing strategy is very simple:

  1. You can use this product internally within your organization and tailor it to your specific needs.
  2. You can't sell it, license it, or otherwise distribute it to others without my permission.
  3. If you tailor it you must keep the original copyright notice on both the pages which you tailor as well as any diagrams which you tailor. If you change a diagram, the copyright notice should read something like "Copyright 2005 YourOrganization, Original Copyright 2005 Scott W. Ambler"
  4. If you find that you are getting value from using this product, I would appreciate a payment of $50 per developer seat.  This is basically the price of a software development book, and I think that you'll agree that I've easily provided far more than that here with this product.  Easiest way to make the donation is to simply pay me via PayPal, otherwise contact me and we'll determine a payment method (e.g. send me a check).
  5. If you're an IT consulting company and you'd like to offer services based on this product, contact me and we'll put together a plan.
  6. If your needs are more complicated than what I've described here, contact me and we'll negotiate licensing terms.

Artifact: Information Needs List (PSM)
The information needs list provides a prioritized list of the information needs applicable to a particular project.
Domains: project_management
Work Product Kinds: assessment
Purpose

This artifact documents the information needs that will be addressed with measurement (see Concept: Measurement (PSM)). It is used to collect information needs from the   Role: Project Manager role and other stakeholders that are input to the Task: Plan Measurement (PSM).  It documents the rationale for measurement.

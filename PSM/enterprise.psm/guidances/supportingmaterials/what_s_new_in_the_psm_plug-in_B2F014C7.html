PSM Plug-in V3.0

This is the first release of the standalone Rational Method Composer plug-in,  There are no dependencies to any other plug-in.

The version number for this plug-in starts at V3.0 because the content was developed under the auspice of the RUP for PSM plug-in, which currently is at release V3.0.  Thus, the V3.0 tag is to reflect that the content in this PSM standalone plug-in reflects the content that is in the RUP for PSM plug-in, without the RUP content, of course.

Role: Measurement Analyst (PSM)
The Measurement Analyst role is primarily responsible for planning measurement, collecting and processing the measurement data, analyzing the measurement results and reporting the results to management.

    Main Description

    The Measurement Analyst role is primarily responsible for the planning, performance, evaluation, and improvement of measurement.

    Staffing
    Skills

    The following skills are recommended to fulfill the Measurement Analyst role:

    • Experience in measurement process and measurement analysis
    • Communication skills
    • Estimation, modeling, and statistical analysis (for advanced measurement)
    • Measurement tool knowledge

    Above all, the personnel responsible for measurement must be able to independently arrive at objective answers and alternatives and be able to provide results directly to the project decision makers.

    Assignment Approaches

    Depending on the size and scope of the project, the measurement team can be a part-time analyst or a multi-person team. In either case, the primary measurement responsibility for the project must be assigned to a specific individual, and that individual must interface directly with the project development organization.

    In a small team, the Measurement Analyst role and the Project Manager roles may be performed by the same person.

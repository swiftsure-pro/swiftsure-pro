Artifact: Measurement Analysis and Recommendations (PSM)
The measurement analysis and recommendations is a report summarizing the results of the measurement analysis. It addresses the analysis of the measurement data, against the information needs list. For any measures where problems or risks are identified, recommended solutions should be identified.
Domains: project_management
Work Product Kinds: assessment
Purpose

This artifact is used to document the measurement analysis and recommendations. It may be used to convey information and recommendations actions to higher-level managers and other project members.

Description
Brief Outline

This report should contain:

  • For each high-priority information need identified, the report should provide a measurement indicator and description of its interpretation.  Any deviations outside the decision criteria should be described, along with alternatives and recommendations on how to alleviate any problems or risks identified.
  • The measurement integrated analysis model should be used to analyze the relationships between measures, and any identified relationships should be described.
  • An overall project analysis should provide an overall evaluation of the project; along with identification of specific problems, risks, and lack of information; recommendations; and any potential new information needs that should be addressed with future measurement analysis reports.
Tailoring
Representation Options

The measurement analysis and recommendations report may be documented in a report, through presentation slides, through wall or web postings, or through staff meetings.

Work Product Descriptor (Artifact): Measurement Analysis and Recommendations (PSM)
The measurement analysis and recommendations is a report summarizing the results of the measurement analysis. It addresses the analysis of the measurement data, against the information needs list. For any measures where problems or risks are identified, recommended solutions should be identified.

Outcome: Work Product Approval
This generic record type defines a formal record of signing off on the quality of one or more work products.
Domains: environment
Work Product Kinds: Record
Extends: Approval
Purpose

To provide formal evidence of the acceptance of one or more work products.

Main Description

Approvals provide accountability. Work product approvals are the final step in ensuring the quality of individual work products. The attributes of the generic Approval record are amplified in these ways:

  • Creator: This role is accountable for the work product and must, therefore, be different from the individual who is responsible for the work product under approval. Typically, this role is assigned to
  • Elements: An approval usually focuses on a single work product, but it can occasionally encompass a list of  work products.
  • Supporting information: This should include the prior approvals for the controlled work products that these work products depended on before this approval.

Key Considerations

Strongly consider tooling opportunities to securely automate the creation of records and store the resulting record, particularly if the project is large or if the team is geographically distributed.

Tailoring
Reasons for not needing

Each organization will need to refine the set of records that it requires to align with its compliance needs and development process. Records may be added or removed in accordance with an organization's policies or process results.

Outcome: Review Record
This abstract work product defines the fundamental properties that all records share.
Domains: environment
Work Product Kinds: Record
Extends: Record
Purpose

For any controlled work product, this record is key to establishing the conditions and participants to the review process


Main Description

For compliance management purposes, the review record of a controlled work product must include:

  • The work product, or list of  work products, that have been reviewed
  • The review stakeholders involved
  • The review comments and associated review decisions recorded

Existing record attributes are amplified as follows:

  • Creator: Says under whose authority the review was conducted
  • Supporting information: Includes the list of  additional supporting work products on which the review was based

The organizer of the review can be virtually anyone (often an author of the document under review), but participants must include the technical authority under whom the work products reviewed falls (the person in the role responsible for the quality of the work product). This is the person responsible for producing the review record and controlling any required follow-up activities.

Key Considerations

Whenever a review is key to documenting compliance, the content and structure of this work product must be formally checked to meet applicable requirements.
Tailoring
Reasons for not needing

Each organization will need to refine the set of records that it requires to align with its compliance needs and development process. Records may be added or removed in accordance with an organization's policies or process results.

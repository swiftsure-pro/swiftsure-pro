Main Description

This approval is a particular type of Work Product Approval. It provides a record of the acceptance of a change request into the development process. Careful consideration must be given regarding which change requests to approve. These considerations include:

  • Priority
  • Architectural significance
  • Effort involved and the schedule
  • Impact on the development work completed to date

 Change Requests are also known by various names, such as CRs, defects, bugs, incidents, and enhancement requests. This approval applies to all of these specific change requests.

Key Considerations

Tooling opportunities to securely automate the creation of records and store the resulting record should be strongly considered, particularly if the project is large or if the team is geographically distributed.

This approval is often conducted by a Change Control Board, or by a Management representative appointed to that role. This approval is essential to keep control of "scope creep," or unintentional expansion of the scope of the project.

Tailoring
Impact of not having

Every project -- compliance-related or not -- should have some kind of approval mechanism in place for change requests.

Reasons for not needing

Each organization will need to refine the set of records that it requires to align with its compliance needs and development process. Records may be added or removed in accordance with an organization's policies or process results.

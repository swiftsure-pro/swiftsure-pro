Main Description

This approval is a particular type of Work Product Approval. It signifies that the subsystem baseline is accepted as the standard, moving forward. This approval could be for further integration or further development activities. Subsystem baselines are progressively integrated to culminate in a system baseline. The level of formality of this baseline approval will increase as the work products mature and the release moves closer to a production environment.

Key Considerations

Strongly consider tooling opportunities to securely automate the creation of records and store the resulting record, particularly if the project is large or if the team is geographically distributed.

Tailoring
Reasons for not needing

Each organization will need to refine the set of records that it requires to align with its compliance needs and development process. Records may be added or removed in accordance with an organization's policies or process results.

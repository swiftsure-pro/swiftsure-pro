Description
Main Description

Compliance regulations are often nondirective, which means that, although most regulations tell you what you need to do, they don't tell you how to do it. The corporate Compliance Policy document defines an organization's interpretation and translation of regulations into a specific set of actionable policies.

Key Considerations

The translation of a regulation into a specific corporate policy can be left open to interpretation. This interpretation is up to the  Compliance Analyst within the organization.

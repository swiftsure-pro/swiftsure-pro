Main Description

This artifact is a Record to be created at project closeout.

At the end of the project, all of the work products useful to maintenance and future development or necessary to support compliance management should be baselined and placed in an archive. This record will encapsulate the work done on the project and will be key in facilitating audits.

Key Considerations
If the work products are stored in multiple repositories, a baseline aggregating the parts of the archive must be created.
Tailoring
Reasons for not needing

Each organization will need to refine the set of records that it requires to align with its compliance needs and development process. Records may be added or removed in accordance with an organization's policies or process results.

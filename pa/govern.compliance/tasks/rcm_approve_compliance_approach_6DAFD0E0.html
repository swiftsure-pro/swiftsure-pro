Task: Approve Compliance Approach
This tasks records the acceptance of the compliance vision addendum.
Disciplines: rup_requirements_discipline
Extends: Approve Work Products
Purpose

To ensure that the core compliance stakeholders accept the approach being used to address the project's compliance objectives. This will include acceptance of the compliance requirements in the scope of the project, as well as acceptance of the timeline for delivery.

Relationships
InputsMandatory:
  • Compliance Requirements
  • Compliance Vision Addendum
    		Optional:
    • None
    Outputs
    • Compliance Requirements Approval
    • Compliance Vision Addendum Approval
      Main Description

      The approval constitutes agreement with the compliance approach. This task is closely coupled with the Task: Formulate Compliance Vision

      This includes:

      • Reviewing the objectives for the project
      • Traceability back to the compliance requirements
      • The timeline

      The person responsible for approving this is a high-level manager vested with compliance responsibility (often the project owner), but that manager will seek input during the review from the stakeholders. These stakeholders will include business representatives and the Compliance Officer. This could occur through a formal review of the artifacts.

      Steps
      Verify entry criteria
      Verify that all prerequisites that are subject to approval for the completion of the work products subject to approval have been met.
      Inspect work product and supporting material
      This step is not a thorough review; it is closer to spot-checking. It should be supported by an appropriate checklist
      Engage concerned parties to resolve concerns

      If the stakeholders identify any issues with the compliance approach, the approver will need to review these with the project team. If the issues are a serious concern, a corrective plan should be put in place. The Approver may decide to postpone or deny approval of the work products until the issues have been agreed upon and addressed.

      Create approval record
      The Approver signs off the changes either by signing a paper copy of the work product or uses an electronic signature when the tooling is available. For electronic signatures, the work order associated with the work product or an electronic reference to the work product is approved by the person in the role that is responsible for this task.
      Key Considerations
      In an iterative cycle, the evolution of the compliance requirements must be factored into this task. This approval may need to be revisited at the end of each iteration or phase to review the approved requirements.

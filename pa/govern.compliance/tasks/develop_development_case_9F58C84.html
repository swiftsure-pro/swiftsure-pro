    Steps
    Review and update compliance management work products

    While compliance management work products tend to be pretty stable, they may have to be customized or simplified on a project-by-project basis. The Quality Assurance team reviews them and makes required changes. This must include:

    • The  Role: Process Engineer reviews and updates the Artifact: Record Control Specification, with special attention to the composition of the Outcome: Project Archive
    • In some rare cases, changes in the CASE tools used may affect process automation, which may, therefore, warrant a review of the Artifact: Process Validation Plan by the Process Engineer.

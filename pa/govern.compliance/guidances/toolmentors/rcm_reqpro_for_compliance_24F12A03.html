Tool Mentor: Requisite Pro for Compliance
This tool mentor outlines the ways in which IBM Rational RequisitePro can support compliance management.
Main Description

RequisitePro provides the requirements and change management aspects of the IBM Rational Software Delivery Platform process solution that support the ability to initiate a Compliance Enhancement Project with the appropriate levels of traceability and control.

RequisitePro empowers software teams to track the definition, design, and implementation of requirements, from requirements elicitation through deployment. The Business Analyst details the compliance enhancement project application requirements, establishes priorities, and establishes a workflow around the in scope requirements within IBM Rational ClearQuest (see Tool Mentor: ClearQuest for Compliance).

The table below highlights some of the key aspects required, and shows how RequisitePro aligns with these needs. Traceability of the process to its implementation through IBM Rational Portfolio Manager is maintained by the export capability of IBM Rational Method Composer.

Term Definition: Business Controls
Main Description

This term characterizes the organization's internal policies, procedures, practices, and organizational structures created by Executive management to make sure that business objectives will be achieved, and that undesired events will be prevented or detected.

Business controls are introduced into IT organizations through Application Control and IT Process Controls.

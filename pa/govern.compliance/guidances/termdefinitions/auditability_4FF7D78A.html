Term Definition: Auditability
Main Description

This term denotes the ability to address the fundamentals of audit expectations.

Process Audit Methodology is based upon the following inspections:

  1. Demonstration of documented process: Ideally, this includes an assessment of the integrity of the associated process being implemented.
  2. Demonstration of process adherence: This is typically validated by examining execution or process logs.
  3. Demonstration of Control over the product or service produced and the quality of the product or service produced: This is typically validated by examining:
    • Metrics and measures of product quality over time
    • Work product traceability on the product or service produced

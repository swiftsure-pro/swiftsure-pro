Checklist: Compliance Vision Addendum Approval
This checklist provides a list of items to review for approving the Compliance Vision Addendum work product.
Extends: Approval
Relationships
Related Elements

  • Compliance Vision Addendum Approval
Check Items
Verify that the versions of controlled work products required (on which the work product(s) is (are) based) are all in an approved state.
Verify that all procedural requirements for producing the work product(s) have been met
This includes conducting reviews, correcting any issues, and involving all stakeholders
Spot-check the checklist for the work product(s) up for approval
Address any concerns with the responsible role
Depending on the concerns, this may require calling a meeting including the responsible role and selected developers and stakeholders.

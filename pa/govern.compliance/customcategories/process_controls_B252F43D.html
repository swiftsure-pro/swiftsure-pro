Main Description

This category describes the additions made to the RUP product to implement Compliance Management controls. It includes

  1. The roadmap describing how these controls have been specified in the RUP product
  2. A set of sub-categories identifying the artifacts, capability patterns, and guidance central to each control. They are:
  • Process management
  • Record control
  • Work product control
  • Control points
  • Traceability

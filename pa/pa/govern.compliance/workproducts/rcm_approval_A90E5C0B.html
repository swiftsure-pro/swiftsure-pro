Outcome: Approval
This generic record type defines the fundamental additional properties that are unique to approval records.
Domains: environment
Work Product Kinds: Record
Extends: Record
Purpose

To provide formal evidence of the acceptance of process activities or work products as satisfactory

Main Description

Approvals record accountability. In addition to generic Record attributes, an approval must include:

  • The set of elements (activities or work products) approved, with version information assigned

Existing record attributes are amplified as follows:

  • Creator: the role and identity of the approver

An approval may also be a control point, where the ownership of one or more work products is passed through an approval and on to another role. An example is the handover point where a developer delivers changes into an integration stream. In that situation, the ownership is passed on to the Integrator.

Key Considerations

Strongly consider tooling opportunities to securely automate the creation of records and store the resulting record, particularly if the project is large or if the team is geographically distributed.

Tailoring
Reasons for not needing

Each organization will need to refine the set of records that it requires to align with its compliance needs and development process. Records may be added or removed in accordance with an organization's policies or process results.

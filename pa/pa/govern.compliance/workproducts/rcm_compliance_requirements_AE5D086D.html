Main Description

In the realm of compliance management, the scope and requirements for compliance initiatives are developed by the Role: Compliance Analyst and managed through a governance framework centered around a portfolio management process. This process is outside of the scope of this plug-in. Therefore, for using this plug-in, the compliance enhancement project comes with an initial set of compliance requirements that serve as a basis for the development the Artifact: Compliance Vision Addendum.

Checklist: Control Point
This base Control Point checklist identifies the checklist items that all control points share.
Check Items
All control point work products (artifacts and records) were produced

This includes both artifacts and records.

To accomplish this step it may be valuable to peruse and spot-check the checklists associated with these work products

The artifacts are structurally consistent
This includes checking traceability, dependencies, and work product versions
The quality of the work products is compatible with the goals of the control point
The same artifact may be reviewed and approved multiple times throughout the life of a project. The goals and quality level of each appoval will vary, and must be verified within the control point.
All decisions made affecting the scope and output of the project are acceptable individually and as a whole
This mainly includes changes to requirements and any related agreements. The goal is that the overall impact of these decisions is acceptable and still keeps the project within its goals.

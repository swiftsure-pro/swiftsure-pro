Content

RUP for Compliance Management is designed to provide guidance for solving the complex issue of managing compliance with government regulations and industry process standards, as well as internal policies and practices. It can assist customers in defining and implementing a compliant software development process that addresses separation of duties, control points, audit reports, and authorization issues.

Critical areas that contribute towards managing compliance for software development include:

  • Ability to establish a compliant development process
    • Approval checkpoints (both project and technical)
    • Quality gates
    • Separation of duties
  • Ability to enforce a compliant development process
    • Automated workflow
    • Authentication
    • Traceability
    • Electronic signature
    • Separation of duties with regard to user rights administration
    • Build management
    • Application security testing and management
  • Ability to monitor and report software development controls status within broader IT-wide and corporate-wide controls management systems

Dependencies

This plug-in depends on the RUP plug-in, and the Formal Resources plug-in.

Usage Note on Processes

This plug-in contributes to processes in the RUP plug-in.  It contributes both steps to tasks, as well as some new tasks to RUP processes.  For these processes to publish properly, the processes need to be synchronized for the added steps to be visible in the process content.

If this synchronization has not been done, then in a published site, tasks that are missing steps will show hollow circles for the steps in the Work Breakdown Structure tab in the 'Steps' column.  To see the missing steps, follow the link to the 'Based on Method Task:' link provided for these tasks.

Welcome to RUP for Compliance Management
Main Description

The IBM® Rational Unified Process® for Compliance Management (RUP for Compliance Management), extends RUP with controls supporting general "Auditability". This includes:

  • Defining compliance management practices (IT Process Controls)
  • Supporting Compliance Enhancement Projects whose purpose is to implement compliance requirements affecting IT systems (Application Control)

The resulting process produces evidence necessary to support an audit. This can help demonstrate compliance to standards and regulations relating to software development. Note that this plug-in does not provide guidance specific to any standard or regulation.

RUP for Compliance Management Futures

This first product release can be used as-is to enable effective compliance management, but it does not provide complete treatment of this domain. Most notably, the following content is currently under investigation:

  1. A process for managing and extending the enhanced RUP to address situation-specific compliance management issues (IT Process Control Development Projects in gray above) is not provided
  2. A comprehensive set of examples for compliance management artifacts
  3. Specific task-based tool-mentors

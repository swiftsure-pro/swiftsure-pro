Term Definition: IT Process Controls
Main Description

The subset of Business Controls embedded in IT processes and services. IT process controls support business processes, and affect IT provided services (for instance, networks, databases, operating systems, and storage).

The reliable operation of these controls is necessary for reliance to be placed on Application Control. For example, poor change management could jeopardize (by accident or deliberate act) the reliability of automated integrity checks.

Other example areas subject to IT controls include:

    • Systems development
    • Change management
    • Security
    • Computer operations

Abstract

This document is intended to support organizations adopting agile practices and want to use IBM® Rational Team Concert® as the collaboration tool for the project teams. We describe how to use the Rational Team Concert process template in conjunction with the process for agile delivery available in IBM® Rational Method Composer®.

Among the benefits of the mapping proposed in this document is the capability of having the work items assigned to team members in Rational Team Concert reflecting the Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) elements (i.e. activities and tasks) of the process found in the IBM Practices for Agile Delivery available in Rational Method Composer. The IBM Practices also provide the process guidance for team members while they are performing their daily tasks. The project plan and iteration plans in Rational Team Concert reflect the look-and-feel of the templates suggested by the IBM Practices content.

Checklist: Adoption Plan
This checklist contains questions to verify that Adoption Plans for capability improvements are created in a consistent manner.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Adoption Plan
Check Items
Have you clearly specified the goals for adoption?
Have you identified adoption risks and barriers?
Have you identified a set of pilot projects and determined how the improvements will be rolled out to the rest of the organization?
Have you established measurements in order to assess the results of adoption?
Have you identified mentoring and training needs?
Have you documented the communication plan for the project?
Have you specified the environment required to support the capability improvements?

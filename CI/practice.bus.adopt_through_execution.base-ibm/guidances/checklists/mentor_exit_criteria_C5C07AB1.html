Checklist: Mentor Exit Criteria
This checklist contains criteria for determining when mentors should exit a project.

Main Description


.
Check Items
Project discipline leads demonstrate self-sufficient behavior
Metrics are trending toward goals
For iterative projects, mentors should be engaged at least through 2 iterations
For projects following the Rational Unified Process, mentors should be engaged at least through elaboration
For waterfall projects, mentors are staged as disciplines are executed
No high impact, high probability risks remained
Artifact reviews demonstrate applied learning of the process and tools deployed
Lessons learned and feedback gathered

Checklist: Mentoring Program Plan
This checklist contains questions to verify that Center of Capability Mentoring Program Plans are created in a consistent manner.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Mentoring Program Plan
Check Items
Are the vision and scope for this Center of Capability clearly defined?
Have procedures for monitoring and controling the program been identified?
Have measurements been identified to monitor performance against identified business objectives?
Is there a staffing model in place describing how to identify and staff mentors?
Has the mentor training path been documented? Are required training assets described?
Is a plan in place to evangelize and socialize the mentoring program?

Role: Center of Capability Coordinator
Manages the Center of Capability and develops mentors to support process and tool adoption on projects.
Role Sets: Software Capability Improvement
Relationships
Primary Performs
  • Develop Mentors
  • Establish the Mentoring Program
  • Manage the Mentoring Program
Modifies
  • Center of Capability Established
  • Developed Mentors
  • Mentoring Program Plan
    Main Description

    This role is responsible for:

    • Establishing the Center of Capability to drive adoption of new process and tools. Creates the vision and mentoring program plan.
    • Planning and strategy for the mentoring program
    • Identifying projects for mentoring
    • Identifying and training mentors
    • Coordinating the schedule of mentors
    • Measuring and reporting the progress and value of the mentoring program to sponsors and stakeholders
    • Managing the intellectual of the Center of Capability

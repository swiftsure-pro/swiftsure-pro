Main Description

This is the equivalent of the RUP's Analysis & Design discipline, the main difference being a much wider area of concern, as a system will include software, hardware and people.

The purposes of Analysis & Design within Systems Engineering are:

  • To transform the requirements into a design of the system-to-be.
  • To evolve a robust architecture for the system, addressing all the relevant views and viewpoints for the system
  • To clearly specify the interfaces between the defined subsystems
  • To create all the prerequisite artifacts needed for subsystem development: vision, use-case model, interface specifications

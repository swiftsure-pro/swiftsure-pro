Abstract

This paper introduces a derivative of the Rational Unified Process that addresses the problem of system specification, analysis, design, and development. The paper provides an overview of the following:

  • RUP principles that are maintained in RUP for Systems Engineering (RUP SE)
  • RUP SE requirements models
  • The UML-based artifacts for system architecture modeling
  • The workflows for creating the artifacts

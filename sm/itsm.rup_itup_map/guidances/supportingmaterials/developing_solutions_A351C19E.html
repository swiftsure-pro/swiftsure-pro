Developing Solution
This page presents at a high-level the commonality, variability, and mapping between RUP and ITUP.
Main Description

RUP is a reference model for the development of software and systems.  ITUP Solution Development processes are used to develop the solutions that provide IT services.  Many of the RUP disciplines overlap with the ITUP Solution Development processes. 



ITUP Process
RUP Discipline

Architecture Management  Business Modeling Architecture Management includes different types of architectures, including business modeling.
Solution Requirements  Requirements equivalent

Solution Acceptance parts of  Project Management Acceptance of solution artifacts at various points in the project
parts of Solution Build  Environment Solution Build only indirectly addresses the development environment
Project Management  Project Management ITUP Project Management encompasses many different types of IT projects, not just development projects. 

In these areas, the RUP disciplines provide a more robust approach.

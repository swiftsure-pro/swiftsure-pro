Term Definition: Definitive Hardware Store (DHS)
Main Description
An area set aside for the secure storage of authorized hardware spares. These are spare components and assemblies that are maintained at the same level as the comparative systems within the live environments. Details of these components and their respective builds and contents should be comprehensively recorded in the Configuration Management Database (CMDB). These can then be used in a controlled manner when needed for additional systems or in the recovery from major incidents. Once their (temporary) use has ended, they should be returned to the DHS or replacements obtained.

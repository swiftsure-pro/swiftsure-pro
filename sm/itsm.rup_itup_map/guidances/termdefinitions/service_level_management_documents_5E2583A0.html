Term Definition: Service Level Management Documents
Main Description

Service Level Management (SLM) documents are a detailed recording of the Customer's needs as depicted in Figure1. The SLM documents are a collection of information intended to represent a blueprint for defining, adapting, and the revising of services.

The list of service level management documents is:

  • Service Level Requirements from customer
  • Negotiations between the SLM process and customer
  • Service Level Agreement (SLA)
  • Detailed Service Requirements 
  • Operational Level Agreement (OLA)
  • Underpinning Contract (UC)

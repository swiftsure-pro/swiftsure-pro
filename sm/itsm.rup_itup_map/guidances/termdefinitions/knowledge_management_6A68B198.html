Term Definition: Knowledge Management
Main Description

Knowledge management is a related set of repeatable activities focused on capturing and managing IT intellectual capital to ensure that the organization's intellectual capital relating to IT is captured, shared, maintained and institutionalized.

Knowledge management is a very broad subject area and not covered as part of this plug-in but it is addressed fully as a process within the  IBM Tivoli Unified (ITUP) IT Administrative process category.

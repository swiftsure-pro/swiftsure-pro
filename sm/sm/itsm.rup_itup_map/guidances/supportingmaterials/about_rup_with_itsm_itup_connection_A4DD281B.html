The IBM® Rational Unified Process® with Information Technology Service Management/IBM Tivoli Unified Process®  Connection (RUP with ITSM/ITUP Connection) is an IBM Rational and IBM Tivoli integrated solution enabling organizations to unify the RUP software development processes and best practices with the IT Service Management guidance within the IBM Tivoli Unified Process®(ITUP®). Additionally, RUP with ITSM/ITUP Connection is tightly aligned with the Information Technology Infrastructure Library® (ITIL®), as is ITUP.

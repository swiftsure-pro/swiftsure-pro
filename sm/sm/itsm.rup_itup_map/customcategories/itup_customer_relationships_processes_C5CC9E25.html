Main Description

The ITUP Customer Relationship processes are:

  • Customer Satisfaction Management
  • IT Customer Transformation Management
  • Service Level Management
  • Service Marketing and Sales
  • Stakeholder Requirements Management

The integration method covers the following process and its corresponding relationship with RUP:

  • The Service Level Management Process Overview highlights the process of planning, coordinating, drafting, agreeing, monitoring, and reporting on Service Level Agreements (SLA) and the ongoing review of service achievements to ensure that the required and cost-justifiable service quality is maintained and gradually improved.

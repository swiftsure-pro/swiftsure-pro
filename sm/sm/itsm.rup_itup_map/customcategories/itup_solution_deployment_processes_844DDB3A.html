Main Description

The ITUP Solution Deployment processes are:

      • Change Management
      • Configuration Management
      • Release Management

 

The integration method covers the following processes and their corresponding relationship with RUP:

  • The Release Management Process Overview describes how to take solutions from having completed testing and achieved accepted status through to being deployed as services in their intended 'live' environment.
  • The Change Management Process Overview describes the process for ensuring that standardized methods and procedures are used for efficient and prompt handling of a Request for Change (RFC), in order to minimize the impact of any related Incident upon service.
  • The Configuration Management Process Overview covers the process of identifying, recording, and reporting of IT components (Configuration Item (CI)), including their versions, constituent components, and relationships These items include hardware, software, and the associated documentation.

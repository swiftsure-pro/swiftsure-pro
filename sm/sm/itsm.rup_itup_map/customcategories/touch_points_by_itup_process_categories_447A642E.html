Main Description

The IBM Tivoli Unified Process (ITUP) describes a comprehensive set of IT Processes within an IT organization and groups these processes into categories which are based on the IBM Process Reference Model for IT® (PRM-IT®). Each of the IT process categories decomposes into a number of processes, as depicted in  Figure 1.



Figure 1 - The Process Reference Model for Information Technology (PRM-IT)

The following ITUP processes categories are the focus of this method and provide content that begins to address the integration between RUP and ITUP. Not all the ITUP processes categories are covered with touch points in this method but may be covered in future releases.

  • The ITUP Customer Relationships Processes contain content that gives the IT service providers a mechanism to understand, monitor, and perform effectively in the marketplace they serve.
  • The ITUP Operational Services Processes contains the operational service processes that describe the daily activity which delivers service from the use of the Infrastructure, Applications and Services in order to meet Service Level agreements and business targets.
  • The ITUP Solution Deployment Processes contain content that describes taking solutions from having completed testing and achieved 'accepted' status through to being deployed as services in their intended 'live' environment.
  • The ITUP IT Resilience Processes describes the analysis and proactive planning required to enable resilient Infrastructure, Applications and Services. Each process covers a range of activities from handling everyday adjustments as required by service operations through anticipating the potential demands upon its specific area of attention.

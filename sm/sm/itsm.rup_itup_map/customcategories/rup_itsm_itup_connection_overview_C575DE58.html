Main Description

The Information Technology Infrastructure Library® (ITIL®) content introduces the Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL®). ITIL is a framework of best practice approaches intended to facilitate the delivery of high quality information technology (IT) services.

The ITUP Method Content Introduction content introduces the IBM Tivoli Unified Process (ITUP) which describes a comprehensive set of IT Processes within an IT organization.

The Overview of the Relationship Between RUP and ITUP content presents RUP and ITUP as process frameworks that emerged from different perspectives, and introduces these process frameworks as related in a number of ways while serving different purposes.

The ITUP Terms content and the RUP and ITUP Terminology Cross Reference content will help you overcome any challenges in the use of new or existing terms.

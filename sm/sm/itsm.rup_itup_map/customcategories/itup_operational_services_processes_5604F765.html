ITUP Operational Services Processes
The Operational Services Process category contains the operational service processes that describe the daily activity which delivers service from the use of the Infrastructure, Applications and Services in order to meet Service Level agreements and business targets.

Main Description

The ITUP IT Operational Services processes are:

    • Data Management
    • Event Management
    • Incident Management
    • Problem Management
    • Service Execution
    • User Contact Management

 

The integration method covers the following process and its corresponding relationship with RUP:

  • The Problem Management Process Overview describes how to minimize the adverse impacts and problems on the business that are caused by errors within the IT infrastructure, and to prevent recurrence of incidents related to these errors.

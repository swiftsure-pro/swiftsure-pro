Task Descriptor: Prioritize Subsystems
This task is where the subsystems are prioritized, so that their order of development or further refinement can be decided.

Main Description

The selection of the subsystems is driven by the following:

  • The benefit of the subsystem to stakeholders: critical, important, useful.
  • The architectural impact of the subsystem: none, extends, modifies.
  • The risks to be mitigated: performance, availability of a product, and suitability of a component.

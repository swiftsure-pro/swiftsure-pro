Estimation Considerations: Architectural Decisions
These estimating guidelines are for the effort of making architectural decisions as part of the ongoing technical issue resolution and architecture coordination.

Main Description

This artifact evolves throughout the project and is largely a by-product of ongoing technical issue resolution and architectural coordination.

Actually making and documenting decisions can take anywhere from minutes for a simple decision to several days if the decision is complex.  Complex decisions may require multiple stakeholder inputs and review meetings to identify options and agree on an approach.

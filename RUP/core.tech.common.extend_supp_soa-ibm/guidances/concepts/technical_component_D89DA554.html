Concept: Technical Component
This concept describes technical components, which support the realization of services by providing infrastructure services support to a service component.

Main Description

Technical, or Infrastructure, components serve to make available horizontal platform capabilities.  The capabilities they provide cut across business domains. Sample functionality provided by technical components includes authentication, error handling, auditing, logging, etc. These technical services are frequently provided by middleware products, including operating systems, and are used either directly by the service component or by the functional components on which they rely.

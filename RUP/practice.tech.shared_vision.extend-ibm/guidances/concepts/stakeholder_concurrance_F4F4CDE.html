Concept: Stakeholder Concurrence
Gain agreement on business problems between stakeholders and development teams early and often.
Relationships
Related Elements
  • Develop Technical Vision
Main Description
Concurrence represents an agreement from the stakeholder regarding requirement assets that are reviewed. In the case of the Vision document these requirement artifacts would include the business problems and proposed solutions to those problems. Obtaining agreement is crucial as it ensures the stakeholder and development team are on the same page. Concurrence can be achieved through a number of techniques. The key point to note is that the ideal is to involve the stakeholder early and often. The development organization must provide updates to the customer at regular intervals where functional code may be demonstrated which resolves a specific need. Concurrence from the stakeholder must be obtained to ensure the team is on track.

  • ServiceChannel (SoaML)
Main Description

In services models, ServiceChannels connect consumers and providers and therefore define the coupling in the system. They isolate the dependencies between consuming and providing participants to particular Request/service interaction points.

This figure illustrates a Manufacturer service Participant that assembles a number of other Participants necessary to actually implement a service as a deployable runtime solution. Manufacturer provides a purchaser service that it delegates to the purchasing service of its orderProcessor part. ServiceChannels connect the Requests to the Services the OrderProcessor needs in order to execute.

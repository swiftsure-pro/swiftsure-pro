Role: Trainer
This role educates and coaches end users on specific topics about the product.
Role Sets: Deployment
Relationships
Primary Performs
  • Conduct Pilot Training
  • Conduct User Training
  • Train the Trainers
Modifies
  • Education and Training Evaluation
  • Instructors Trained
    Main Description
    This role is responsible for delivering training to users in order to prepare them to use the product (result of the project) and become proficient in using the product, in such a way that the project team can transfer the ownership of the product to the customer.
    Staffing
    SkillsA person performing this role needs to have excellent verbal and non-verbal communication skills, and the ability to teach concepts and ideas, and illustrate them with real-life examples preferably based on his or her own experience.
    Assignment ApproachesSome companies have in-house trainers, some prefer to hire training companies to deliver the training needed by a project or enterprise.
    Key Considerations

    Training may be first delivered to a test audience so the trainer can collect feedback and make adjustments to the training material before rolling out the training to other users.

    For scalability purposes, training may be delivered to other trainers first, who will then educate a large population of end users.

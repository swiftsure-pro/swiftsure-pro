Role Descriptor: Test Analyst
This role identifies and defines the required tests, monitors detailed testing progress and results in each test cycle and evaluates the overall quality. The role also represents stakeholders who do not have direct or regular representation on the project.

Main Description

Roles organize the responsibility for performing tasks and developing work products into logical groups. Each role can be assigned to one or more people, and each person can fill one or more roles. When staffing the Test Analyst role, you need to consider both the skills required for the role and the different approaches you can take to assigning staff to the role.

In some development cultures this role is called the Test Designer, or considered a specialization of the Tester role.

Role: Stakeholder
This role represents interest groups whose needs must be satisfied by the project. It is a role that may be played by anyone who is (or potentially will be) materially affected by the outcome of the project.

Main Description

Interested parties have needs and insights into how an organization works, an organization's goals, or what the organization needs to provide to other parties such as customers. This makes the role a subject matter expert in the specific areas of the business they have an interest in.

Staffing
Skills

An understanding of one or more of the following:

  • How the business or organization operates,
  • What the organization needs to provide its stakeholders,
  • The needs of the stakeholders who interact with the organization,
  • How customers interact with the organization.

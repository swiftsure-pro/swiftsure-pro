Artifact: Education and Training Evaluation
The Education & Training Evaluation work product specifies the management, delivery, and evaluation of the training of management, staff, and other stakeholders.
Domains: Deployment
Purpose

This work product provides for the delivery and evaluation of training, including training staff to deliver training, process knowledge, skills, job and role and functional training.  The delivery and evaluation of education and training is key to preparing management and staff for their roles in the new organization and to enable them to use and understand new systems, processes, and ways of working. The evaluation should ensure that this training has been successfully delivered and the requisite skills achieved.
